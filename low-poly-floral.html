Low-Poly Floral Illustration

Low-Poly Floral: A high-resolution, geometric vector illustration. This piece was created by manually mapping and rendering hundreds of polygonal intersections to transform a standard floral image into a stylized, 3D-like aesthetic utilizing Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

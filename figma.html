Terrapin Eats

Figma UI/UX Prototype

About the Project

A high-fidelity Figma prototype of a mobile application designed to tailor food interests to UMD students. It provides accessible local food recommendations by filtering options based on interests and proximity, complete with a simulated interactive map and a coupon redemption interface.
